Frequently Asked Questions

Let's answer some of the questions you may have.

About LeafBark
What is LeafBark?
LeafBark is a real estate investment marketplace that allows investors to buy equity shares of rental apartments in major cities.
How does it work?
LeafBark finds and "slices" an apartment’s ownership into multiple equal equity shares depending on the property funding required. Each share owns an equity interest in one apartment, and only one apartment. Shares can be purchased by individual investors.
Who owns the apartments?
Each apartment is registered under its own (Special Purpose Vehicle) company. So technically you as an investor own the property in a form of a share.
What am I actually investing in?
You are investing in a single apartment unit, the ownership of which has been divided into multiple equity shares according to the required funding. For example, if you buy 10,000 shares in a property that is divided into 100 000 shares, you would own 10% of that apartment’s stock.
About Properties
What kind of properties are available through LeafBark?
High quality apartments in major cities, not only the types of properties that we aspire to own and live in but also those that have a great investment potential.
Who lives in the apartments that you acquire?
The apartments will be rented to a traditional market-rate tenant.
How do you select properties?
We make use of data-driven technology to target certain cities that have extraordinary catalysts for appreciation potential. We then seek out apartment units in our favorite buildings that we can acquire at a price that makes sense.
Who handles the property management?
We partner with experienced property management companies that uses technology to manage the properties. These companies make use of the technology to handle all the functions including: rental appraisals and reviews, sourcing quality tenants (advertising properties, screening and selecting tenants), minimizing vacancy rates, creating and managing leasing arrangements as well as rental collections.
What is the strategy for the properties and when is a property sold?
We intend to manage the properties as traditional apartment rentals, rented out to market-rate tenants through 12-month leases. We intend to hold the properites for a long term period. However,each property will be reviewed after 5-10 years for its performance and value. If the property is sold the sale proceeds will be distributed to investors.

You’ll have the opportunity to liquidate your property shares on the Resale Market if you wish to exit an investment.
Investing through LeafBark
How do I buy shares?
Buying shares is easy with LeafBark. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Click the 'explore' button on the navigation bar or 'start investing' on the homepage
  2. Browse and choose your desired property investment
  3. Click 'more details' to review the offering and click 'invest'
  4. Enter the number of shares you would like to purchase and confirm
  5. Pay through our payment gateway partner, or deposit money into your wallet and pay
  6. Become an owner!
Note: your account should be verified and your banking details should be updated before you make an investment.
What is the minimum investment?
The minimum investment amount varies for each property. This amount is equal to one full share value.
