About LeafBark
What is LeafBark?
LeafBark is a real estate investment marketplace that allows investors to buy equity shares of rental apartments in major cities.
How does it work?
LeafBark finds and "slices" an apartmentâ€™s ownership into multiple equal equity shares depending on the property funding required. Each share owns an equity interest in one apartment, and only one apartment. Shares can be purchased by individual investors.
Who owns the apartments?
Each apartment is registered under its own (Special Purpose Vehicle) company. So technically you as an investor own the property in a form of a share.
