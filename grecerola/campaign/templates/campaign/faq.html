Frequently Asked Questions

Let's answer some of the questions you may have.

About LeafBark
What is LeafBark?
LeafBark is a real estate investment marketplace that allows investors to buy equity shares of rental apartments in major cities.
How does it work?
LeafBark finds and "slices" an apartment’s ownership into multiple equal equity shares depending on the property funding required. Each share owns an equity interest in one apartment, and only one apartment. Shares can be purchased by individual investors.
Who owns the apartments?
Each apartment is registered under its own (Special Purpose Vehicle) company. So technically you as an investor own the property in a form of a share.
What am I actually investing in?
You are investing in a single apartment unit, the ownership of which has been divided into multiple equity shares according to the required funding. For example, if you buy 10,000 shares in a property that is divided into 100 000 shares, you would own 10% of that apartment’s stock.
Is LeafBark registered and regulated?
LeafBark is a product of Upsitec (PTY) LTD (reg no: 2018/367038/07). LeafBark is a real estate crowdfunding platform. Crowdfunding platforms are not yet directly regulated in South Africa. However, our platform is in compliance with the existing relevant legislation. i.e The Financial Services Conduct Authority, the Consumer Protection Act, Protection of Personal Information Act as well as the Companies Act.
About Properties
What kind of properties are available through LeafBark?
High quality apartments in major cities, not only the types of properties that we aspire to own and live in but also those that have a great investment potential.
Who lives in the apartments that you acquire?
The apartments will be rented to a traditional market-rate tenant.
How do you select properties?
We make use of data-driven technology to target certain cities that have extraordinary catalysts for appreciation potential. We then seek out apartment units in our favorite buildings that we can acquire at a price that makes sense.
Who handles the property management?
We partner with experienced property management companies that uses technology to manage the properties. These companies make use of the technology to handle all the functions including: rental appraisals and reviews, sourcing quality tenants (advertising properties, screening and selecting tenants), minimizing vacancy rates, creating and managing leasing arrangements as well as rental collections.
What is the strategy for the properties and when is a property sold?
We intend to manage the properties as traditional apartment rentals, rented out to market-rate tenants through 12-month leases. We intend to hold the properites for a long term period. However,each property will be reviewed after 5-10 years for its performance and value. If the property is sold the sale proceeds will be distributed to investors.

You’ll have the opportunity to liquidate your property shares on the Resale Market if you wish to exit an investment.
Investing through LeafBark
How do I buy shares?
Buying shares is easy with LeafBark. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Click the 'explore' button on the navigation bar or 'start investing' on the homepage
  2. Browse and choose your desired property investment
  3. Click 'more details' to review the offering and click 'invest'
  4. Enter the number of shares you would like to purchase and confirm
  5. Pay through our payment gateway partner, or deposit money into your wallet and pay
  6. Become an owner!
Note: your account should be verified and your banking details should be updated before you make an investment.
What is the minimum investment?
The minimum investment amount varies for each property. This amount is equal to one full share value.
Can I invest in multiple properties at the same time?
Yes. In fact that is what we highly recommend. By buying shares in different properties, you are diversifying your investment portfolio. This helps you reduce the risk involved.In addition, it allows you to build a balanced portfolio which could protect you against any market unpredictability.
What happens if LeafBark does not raise the full offering amount before a closing date?
When investors buy shares in a property that reaches the full offering target, settlement can take place. If not enough investors purchase Property shares by the funding date then the property is withdrawn and investors monies are returned to their account.
How long do I have to keep my investment for?
There is no term or time restrictions for how long you can own shares for.
Where are my funds held?
Prior to the closing of a property offering, funds will be held in a trust account separate from LeafBark's business operating account. Upon closing, the funds will be used to acquire the property.
 Why is there a discrepancy between the purchase price and the offering amount?
The offering amount includes operating and capital reserves, closing costs, and offering costs.
Is my personal information secure?
We do the best we can to keep your information safe. The platform is built with high-end data encryption, secure payments processes and we conduct regular testing to check and improve our platform security.
How you make money
How does my investment with LeafBark earn a return?
LeafBark investments offers both capital gain opportunities and rental returns:

Monthly Rental Returns:
As a shares owner you will receive your share of the net rental income. Rent is collected from each property's tenants and shared proportionately among the investors who have shares in that property (less the Property Management fee and any costs). Your share of net rental income is paid directly into your wallet each month. You can either withdraw your monthly rental income or reinvest them to build your portfolio.

Capital gains:
If the property's value goes up, or down, so does your share value. Each property's performance is tracked using area data and independent property valuations. This information is used as a price guide for investors. When the time is right you can sell your shares by offering them to other potential buyers on the platform.
Are my returns taxable?
We will not make any tax deductions as LeafBark. However, any type of gain usually attracts income tax while a capital investment attracts capital gain tax in South Africa. Please do your own research or refer to a tax advisor about tax implications of owning an asset in South Africa.
What can I do with my monthly dividends?
It's up to you. You can either withdraw your monthly rental dividends or reinvest them to build your portfolio(recommeded).
Fees/Costs
What are LeafBark Fees?
You pay 1.5% on the purchase of shares, this fee will be embeded on the property offering. e.g. if each share price R450 initially, R6.75 will be added for service fee and the net share price will be R456.75. 1.5% fee will also be added when you sell the share on the secondary marketplace but will be covered by the buyer.

2% service fee will be deducted from the monthly dividends.

In addition, LeafBark will retain any interest that is earned on investor funds in the trust account for the period that they are held. This interest is retained as an administration fee.

LeafBark fees may change at any time. However, they will always be in the best interest of the investors and we will inform our investors 30 days in advance of any change in fees.
What are the property management company's fees?
Property Management Companies charge 2.5% of the rent to provide their management services. The fee covers a full "all-in" service. Any deviation from this fee will be clearly identified in any offers placed on the platfrom.
