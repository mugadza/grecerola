How it works

Managing repairs

A maintenance fund is raised for potential repairs and maintenance issues. This is then topped up from rent payments. Repairs are handled efficiently to ensure properties are always well maintained.

Tenants

The property management companies collect the rent. All expenses including the property management fees are deducted and then forwarded to LeafBark for distribution to the PropertyShare owners.

Returns

Tenants access secure long term tenancies that are well maintained. They have the option to buy PropertyShares and progress into ownership.
